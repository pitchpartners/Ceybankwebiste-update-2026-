# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 06 January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **CEYBANK** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the **Personal Data Protection Act, No. 9 of 2022 of Sri Lanka (PDPA)** when you access or use our business website **[www.ceybank.com](http://www.ceybank.com)** (the "Site"). By using this Site, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

### a. Personal Data You Provide

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Information submitted through contact forms
* Information provided when subscribing to newsletters or marketing communications

### b. Automatically Collected Data

* IP address
* Browser type and device information
* Pages visited, referring URLs, and usage statistics
* Date and time of access

We use cookies and third-party analytics tools such as **Google Analytics** and **Facebook Pixel** to understand website usage, improve performance, and support marketing activities. You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some website features may not function properly.

Your personal data is collected and processed for lawful purposes under the Sri Lanka PDPA, including:

* Operating and managing the CEYBANK business website
* Responding to inquiries submitted via contact forms
* Sending newsletters and marketing communications (where consent is provided)
* Improving website content, functionality, and user experience
* Monitoring and analyzing website usage and trends
* Ensuring website security and preventing unauthorized access
* Complying with legal and regulatory obligations

## 3. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on one or more of the following lawful grounds:

* Your consent
* Performance of a contract or service request
* Compliance with legal obligations
* Legitimate interests, where applicable and not overridden by your rights

CEYBANK does not sell or rent personal data. We may disclose personal data only in the following circumstances:

* To trusted service providers such as website hosting, analytics, and email communication providers, who process data on our behalf
* When required by applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes

Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable law.
